Showcase

The showcase is a list of tested and supported sites on the Exokit engine. The list is a work in progress, the goal is for the Exokit engine to support any XR site!

Exokit runs regular http(s):// sites with regular web technologies like WebGL/WebXR.

  • Soundboxing WebVR by Eric Florenzano (with Supermedium)

    A VR music kickboxing game!

  • Supercraft by Supermedium

    Build VR in VR with your hands.

  • Musical Forest by Google

    Join users from around the world in a musical forest.

  • Towermax Fitness by Towermax Fitness

    Soundbased virtual reality workout for boxers, martial arts and fitness enthusiasts.

  • Inside Music by Google (with Song Exploder, Supermedium)

    What if you could step inside a song? This is a simple experiment that explores that idea. See and hear the individual layers of music all around you to get a closer look at how music is made.

  • BeatSaver Viewer by Supermedium

    Preview community-created beatmaps for Beat Saber on the Web.

  • CERN CMS / LHC Tour by CERN

    A 360-degree tour of CERN's Large Hadron Collider (LHC) particle acclerator and its Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS).

  • 2019 Chevrolet Silverado by Chevrolet

    A product page and configurator for Chevrolet's 2019 Silverado truck.

  • Hidden Mickey by Disney

    Hidden Mickeys have been slipped in all across Disney. They now find their way into this official A-Frame game, can you spot them?

  • 2019 Ford Expedition by Ford

    Explore the interior of a 2019 Ford Expedition SUV.

  • Hubs by Mozilla

    A new way to get together.

  • Access Mars by NASA (with Google, Supermedium)

    The real surface of Mars. Recorded by NASA's Curiosity rover. Now in your browser.

  • Stand at the Edge of Geologic Time by NPR

    Transport yourself to Rocky Mountain National Park, with all its sights and sounds, in this immersive geology lesson.

  • Samsung VR by Samsung

    Samsung's official immersive 360 content library.

  • Escape Room Movie Challenge by Sony

    $1m sweepstakes challenge from Sony to market the Hollywood film, "Escape Room". Find the clues, escape the rooms.

  • 2019 Toyota Camry by Toyota

    Product page and configurator for the 2019 Toyota Camry.

  • A-Painter by A-Frame Team

    Paint in VR with your hands.
